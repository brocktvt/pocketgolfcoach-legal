Frequently Asked Questions How do I upload a swing for analysis? From the home screen, tap "Analyze Swing", select a video from your library or record a new one, trim it to the relevant swing, and tap Upload. Analysis typically completes within a few minutes. How many analyses do I get on the free tier? Free accounts include 2 swing analyses per month. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited analyses. How do I manage or cancel my subscription? Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store. Go to your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your Pocket Golf Coach subscription. How do I restore a purchase on a new device? Open the app, go to the paywall screen, and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. My analysis seems to have failed — what should I do? Try uploading the video again. Make sure your video clearly shows the full swing and is well-lit. If the issue persists, contact us at the email above and we'll investigate.
